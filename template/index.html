About Project

Today many people are invested in the stock market who invests in stock markets. They are usually unaware of the stock market behavior. Investors are facing the problem of stock trading as they do not know which stocks to buy and which to sell in order to gain more profits. When it is constructing and selecting a portfolio for investment, evaluation of its expected returns and risks is considered the bottom line.

Stock markets depend on high demands, high-demand stocks will increase prices, and heavily sold stocks will decrease prices. The programming language used to predict the stock market is Python using machine learning. The algorithm used is the LSTM (Long-Short Term Memory) network. The stock market is a type of recurrent network that has proved very successful in a number of problems given its capability to distinguish between recent and early examples by giving different weights for each forgetting memory it considers irrelevant to predict the next output. In the share market, prices are not fixed; they change daily.

Stock plays an important role in investment portfolios. The stock market opens at 09:00 am and closes at 03:00 pm. Technology has also been an important factor for selling and buying trades; there are so many mobile apps available.

The main objectives of this application are:

  • To predict the market performance of (SEBI) on day closing using different machine learning techniques.
  • To understand recent technology used for Prediction Purpose in Share Market.
  • To trade the stock market prices for individual investment.
  • To save income by marketing a profit.
  • This Proposed system will analyze stock with respective factors.
  • It particularly focuses on the open, close, high, low value of previous data.
  • Stock Market prices are volatile in nature and are affected by factors like inflammation and economic growth
  • The price of a share market depends on heavily demand and supply. High-demand stocks will increase the price, whereas heavily sold stocks will decrease in price.
